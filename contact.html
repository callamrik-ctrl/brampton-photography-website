Wedding photography FAQ.

How early should we book a wedding photographer?

Most couples book 8 to 14 months in advance for popular GTA wedding dates. Short-notice dates are sometimes available.

Do you photograph South Asian weddings?

Yes. Multi-day wedding coverage can include mehndi, sangeet, maiyan, ceremony, reception, family portraits, and couple portraits.

Do packages include edited photos?

Yes. Packages include professionally edited high-resolution images delivered through a private online gallery.

Can we add video or a second photographer?

Yes. Video, second photographer coverage, albums, and additional hours can be added to most wedding packages.
